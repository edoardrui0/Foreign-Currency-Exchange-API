Enter two currencies to return real-time exchange rate data updated every 10 minutes

Enter a date to see the exchange rate data between two currencies during those dates

The conversion exhcange rate feature is useful for vacations or when you're paying someone using a different currency. Simply input your own currency, then that of the of currency you wish to convert to!

Like the hisotrical feature, the Time-Series allows you to see different exchange rates, but in between two dates, maximum of 365 days apart.
